Here are the answers to some common queries about our Ayurvedic services and treatments.

What is Ayurveda?

Ayurveda is an ancient holistic treatment technique that began in India more than 5,000 years ago. It aims to create balance and harmony in the body, mind, and spirit using natural treatments such as herbal cures, dietary changes, and lifestyle routines.

What kinds of treatments do you offer?

We provide a variety of authentic Ayurvedic treatments, including Panchakarma detoxification therapies, herbal medicine consultations, and Ayurvedic massage therapies like Abhyanga and Swedan.

Are your practitioners qualified?

Yes, our practitioners are highly educated and experienced Ayurvedic doctors and therapists who maintain the highest levels of care and professionalism.

Do you offer online consultations?

Yes, we provide online consultations for those who are unable to visit our clinic in person. You can get the advantages of Ayurveda from the comfort of your home.
